Projects

The Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) Expert Group on Clean Fossil Energy (EGCFE) promotes clean fossil fuels and technologies through a number of projects. These projects are developed in accordance with EGCFE priorities and initiatives, reflect the directions of APEC Energy Ministers, and create tangible benefits for people living in the Asia-Pacific region.

Final reports from many EGCFE projects are available on the APEC website and are linked below. Visitors who are interested in obtaining past reports that are not available online may contact the EGCFE Chair.
